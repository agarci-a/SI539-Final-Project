Beetlejuice Beetlejuice

Synopsis: Still haunted by Beetlejuice, Lydia's life gets turned upside down when her daughter discovers a portal to the afterlife. When someone says Beetlejuice's name three times, the mischievous demon soon returns to unleash his very own brand of mayhem


Release date: September 6, 2024
Director: Tim Burton
Genre: Comedy, Horror
Cast: Winona Ryder, Michael Keaton, Jenna Oretega, and more
Rating: PG-13

my review

Beetlejuice Beetlejuice is one of the most recent films I've watched which is the sequel to the first Beetlejuice movie made in 1988. I really enjoyed it! I thought it was funny and entertaining, and I loved watching Lydia and her stepmother's relationship grow compared from the first movie. I watched it twice when it first came out in September of this year and it was a great movie for the fall and halloween season. I loved the actors, the acting, and directing

my rating: 4.2 / 5 ⭐
